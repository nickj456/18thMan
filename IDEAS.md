# 18th Man — Product Ideas

A running list of ideas for future consideration. Not prioritised, not committed to — just captured.

---

## Collaboration

### Coach Groups
Allow coaches to form groups so they can share session plans privately with each other. Useful for coaching staffs at a single club, or regional development groups.

### Club Lockdown Mode
Option to restrict the platform (or a sub-section of it) to a specific club only — invite-only access, club-specific content, private drill libraries.

---

## Planning & Scheduling

### Calendar View
A calendar view for a coach (or coaching group) showing all planned sessions across the coming weeks. Makes it easy to see training load, gaps, and upcoming fixtures at a glance.

---

## Content & Drills

### Club-Specific Drills / Moves Section
A private drill section where a club can name drills and plays using their own internal terminology — e.g. what they call "The Scissors" at their club. Could sit behind a paid tier. Keeps proprietary moves private to the club.

---

## Monetisation

### Paid Tier
Monthly or yearly subscription to unlock premium features. Possible candidates for paid-only access:
- Club-specific drills / Moves section
- Coach groups / shared session plans
- Club lockdown mode
- Advanced analytics or session history

### PDF Export
Generate a clean, printable PDF of a session plan — layout, drill order, durations, coaching notes. Useful for handing to assistant coaches or printing for the training ground.
