Drill Designer — Visual Canvas Feature

Rugby League-specific field canvas with drag-and-drop elements. Desktop-optimised (also accessible on tablet).

What it does

A browser-based canvas where coaches draw up drills visually — like a digital whiteboard with a rugby pitch background. Coaches place players, draw movement paths, add equipment, and annotate — then save, share, or export the diagram.

Canvas Elements

Players

Drag attacker/defender circles. Number or label each one. Colour-coded (red = attack, blue = defence)

Movement

Draw straight or curved arrows for player runs. Dashed lines for ball passes. Dotted for optional moves

Equipment

Cones, tackle bags, pads, poles. Click to place on field, drag to reposition

Ball

Rugby ball icon to mark starting position and passing sequences

Text Labels

Add coaching cues, phase numbers, zone labels anywhere on the pitch

Zones

Draw shaded rectangles to highlight areas of the field (e.g. red zone, kick zone)

Pitch Backgrounds

🏉 Full pitch
📐 Half pitch
⬜ Blank grid
🎯 In-goal area

Save / Export / Share

💾 Save as drill (linked to drill library)
🖼️ Export as PNG/PDF
🔗 Share direct link
📋 Attach to session plan
Technology recommendation

Konva.js (React Konva) — a performant HTML5 Canvas library with first-class React support. Used by professional drawing tools, handles touch events well for future tablet use, and supports undo/redo natively. This is a desktop-first feature but will be tablet-accessible.
