## Week 3 — Coaching Value

> *Stop selling. Give coaches something useful. 18th Man mentioned naturally — never forced.*

### Monday — Coaching Tip

**Facebook Group Post** — Pillar: T
> Three contact warm-up drills that work at any level (u14s through to open age):
>
> **1. Shoulder pad tag**
> Two players, one pad. The attacker runs at the defender with the pad, defender has to stay square and drive through the contact. Good for getting the body ready and reminding defenders to stay on their feet.
>
> **2. One-on-one tackle technicals**
> Walk pace. Focus on head position, wrap, drive. Don't let players get sloppy just because it's slow. That's the point.
>
> **3. Ruck ball presentation**
> Three players. One carrier, one tackler, one support. Carrier goes to ground correctly, support player presents the ball cleanly. Run it until it's automatic.
>
> All three of these are saved in the 18th Man drill library if you want to grab them and adapt for your squad. Free to access at 18thman.app.
