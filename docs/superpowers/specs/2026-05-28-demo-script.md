# 18th Man — Presentation Demo Script
**Tonight's coaching presentation**

---

## The Structure (20–25 min)

Five beats. Each one builds on the last. End on the sign-up page.

---

## Beat 1 — The Problem (2 min)

**What to say:**
> "How many of you plan your sessions in your head, or on a notepad the night before training? Or you've got a folder of printed drills from 10 years ago?
>
> There are no good tools built for rugby league coaches. Everything out there is generic sports software or designed for union. I built 18th Man to fix that."

**What to show:**
- The landing page: `18thman.app`
- Scroll slowly — let them read the hero: *"Better sessions. Better players."*
- Don't linger. Move quickly to the app.

---

## Beat 2 — AI in Action (5 min) ★ WOW MOMENT

**What to say:**
> "I'm going to show you the thing that gets coaches the most. This is the AI coaching assistant — it understands rugby league. Not just general sport. The sets, the structures, the positions."
>
> *[Type the prompt live — don't pre-load it, let them watch it generate]*
>
> "I'll ask it to plan me a session right now."

**What to do:**
1. Open the app → Dashboard → **Ask AI Coach**
2. Type this prompt live (copy if needed):
   > *"Plan a 60-minute training session for an U14s side, 12 players, focusing on handling under pressure. Give me a full timed run sheet with drills and coaching points."*
3. Let the response stream — don't skip it
4. Scroll through the output: drill names, timing, coaching cues

**Talking point while it streams:**
> "This isn't ChatGPT with 'rugby' bolted on. It knows what a set of six looks like, it knows the Game Sense approach, it knows what U14s can and can't do."

---

## Beat 3 — The Season Sorted: Coaching Blocks (6 min)

**What to say:**
> "That's a one-off session. But what about your whole pre-season block? What about making sure you're not running the same attack drill three weeks in a row?
>
> This is Coaching Blocks. You name the block, set the number of sessions, and AI plans every single one upfront — balanced across Attack, Defence, Completions and Skills."

**What to show:**
1. Navigate to **Groups** → open a group → **Coaching Blocks**
2. Show an existing block — point out the session list with categories colour-coded
3. Open a session — show the AI-generated focus area, drills, and preparation notes
4. **Key point to land:** *"A week before each session, you open it, review the plan, set the date. If the team struggled on the weekend you can swap sessions — the rotation stays intact."*

**Optional:** Show the squad page — player list, notes, ratings per player.

---

## Beat 4 — Match Day: Game Stats (5 min)

**What to say:**
> "Now the match. You want data on your players — not just a score, but who's carrying, who's tackling, whether your set completion is holding up under pressure."

**What to show:**
1. Navigate to **Groups** → group → **Game Stats**
2. Open an existing session in **Tally mode** — show the player list and stat buttons (carry / tackle / set)
3. Tap a few events to demonstrate the live feel
4. Switch to **Review mode** — show the workload % bars, try/conversion scoring, total counts per player
5. Mention the PDF export — *"You can export this after the match."*

**Talking point:**
> "This runs on your phone on the sideline. One tap per event. No paper, no trying to remember after the final siren."

---

## Beat 5 — The Close (3 min)

**What to say:**
> "There's more in here — a drill designer where you draw your drills on a digital pitch canvas, a community board where coaches share what's working at training, squad management, player notes.
>
> But you get the idea. This is built for us.
>
> I've set everyone up with free access through the club. Here's how to get on."

**What to show:**
- Navigate to `18thman.app/coaches`
- Leave this on screen
- Say: *"Scan the QR code or go to 18thman.app/signup. Takes two minutes."*

---

## Tips

- **Don't rush Beat 2.** The AI streaming live is your best moment. Let the room watch it appear.
- **Have a session already open** before Beat 3 so you're not clicking through loading states.
- **Demonstrate on a big screen** — the dark UI looks great projected.
- **End on `/coaches`** — don't navigate away from it. Leave it on screen while you take questions.
